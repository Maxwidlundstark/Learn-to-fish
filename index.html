About Us


We're a small little family businness located on the countryside of the beautiful northern Sweden. We offer regular courses throughout the year for everyone interesting in fishing. In this new course you will learn everything that you need to know when it comes to fishing, regardless of you have previous experience or not.


By joining us you will learn:


  • The Best techniques for different species.
  • How to navigate on water with sea maps.
  • What lures to use.
  • Where to look for fish.
  • How to take care of wildlife & nature.
